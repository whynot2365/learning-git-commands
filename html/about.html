Emmanuel.O, first started My project.com to bring awareness to the fact that, the creation and explosive growth of the internet over the last two odd decades have been the most significant technological development in recent times. The internet has changed the way we read, travel, shop and entertain ourselves. As with most new technologies, it is young people who are in the vanguard of this “netiziation” of our social existence. Not only do youth feel comfortable using the net they also bring with them their irreverence and questioning minds, thus pushing the limits of this medium, using it for things that were scarcely imaginable. Given the widespread interest in this medium amongst our so called “target audience” and in order to tap into this exciting interface between young people, youth culture and the internet, (index.html) organizes a fight and maximize the potential of young people and youth workers who are ready to use the internet for drug abuse prevention.for this reason i took up the burden of learning thoroughly the maximized use of WEB-DESIGN to teach and prevent youths from dabbling into activities that could endager their lives and deprive them the opportunity to manifest Great potential in this field.
